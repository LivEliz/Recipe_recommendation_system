About Our Recipe Site

Welcome to CookSmart, an intelligent recipe recommendation system designed to make cooking easier and more enjoyable. It allows users to explore, search, and manage a variety of recipes, providing step-by-step instructions, ingredient lists, and personalized suggestions. With features like “Top Recipes” and favorites tracking, CookSmart helps both novice and experienced cooks discover new dishes, plan meals efficiently, and share their culinary creations effortlessly.
